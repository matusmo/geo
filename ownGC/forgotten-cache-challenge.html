EARLY BEGINNINGS
First geocaches in Slovakia appeared just one year+ after Stash #1, "published" at personal websites, later at subpage of "bike and GPS" web portal, administered by Lukic. Things started to happen in summer 2002: geocacher JoHnY registered domain geocaching.sk and designed unique form for submissions of new geocaches into the system. There are two admins of "blue web" - JoHnY and Tomco, new cachers and submissions are comming very shortly, game is starting.
Dozens of caches were hidden during first years by more and more players. But here came a trouble ... Geocaching.sk run in parallel with official global site Geocaching.com. Owners used to publish their listings at both sites (slovak listing looks like this) - it ment double work, double databases, double logs. Usually in english on .com, and the same content in slovak on .sk. What is even worse, more and more caches were published just in one database. It started to be pretty chaotic.
Luckily (in oposite to Poles and Hungarians) we've been able to agree on solution once and forever. At beginning of 2006, new slovak geocaching website is introduced by Tomco, Igi and Bubbles.sk with new key feature - database of geocaches is periodically mirrored from geocaching.com. Finally we have finetuned map! And extended, organized info about caches and players, brilliant tool for planning and printing called skrysotlac - CachePrinter, statistics, smart searching, user-friendly forums, news and plenty other useful features and functionalities. (Waste majority of them does not work any longer due to restrictions on geocaching.com side.)
Thanks to cachers like Luki캜, JoHnY, Tomco_sk, Bubbles.sk, Igi and many early owners is geocaching in Slovakia so popular today. May their effort won't be forgotten!
